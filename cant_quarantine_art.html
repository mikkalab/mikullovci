Can't Quarantine ART

First Virtual Exhibition in Kosovo
/ curated exhibition

The exhibition "CAN'T QUARANTINE ART" is an exhibition of first and second year students at the
Department of Graphic Design and Visual Arts - AAB Faculty of Arts.
The works are realized during the quarantine period, in engagements and online assignments in the subjects
"Visual Perception" and "Typographic Posters" led by prof. Besim Mikullovci.
