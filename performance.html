Write and Rip / WAR

Intermedia Performance / besim mikullovci

The performance reflects the real social situation of a country in transition. invasions between different classes of
people are reflected by two characters: leader and low-class person who, in poetry "write and rip" acquainted
with the elephant and mouse.


performers: lulzim bucolli, muhamed arifi, adriana morina, doresa rexha, besart daka, fatlume bunjaku, vjosa malokaj
assistant director ben apolloni, photography alban ramadani, video shooting: mumin jashari, soundman / jetmir rama
concept, video & sound fx besim mikullovci
