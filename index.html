A few words about me

My artistic career spans over three decades across a variety of media, people and places. Throughout, I've developed unique style in graphic  design and motion design in  particular.  My work appears in  book and magazine design, 3D animation, video and film production.  I hold a master’s degree in multimedia at the Faculty of Arts in Prishtina and i'm currently  involved with AAB College in Prishtina, where I teach graphic design, animation & fashion design illustration. I'm also employed by the main public channel, the Radio Television of Kosovo, as an Artistic Advisor.
