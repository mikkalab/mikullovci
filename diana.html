About the project

In Diana's community there was a tradition for girls to get married at a very young age. She had to interrupt her education and get married very young.
Diana’s marriage was a really nightmare because of many problems with her husband. Her son experienced the situation very bad therefore the divorce was more than necessary. Immediately after the divorce, Diana continued with education -first secondary school and later attended University Studies. Her son is a very successful student, and she is very happy about it. Actually she is Finance / Administrator in the Network of Roma, Ashkali and Egyptian Women Organization of Kosovo and recently started her own business. She would like to be a model for all women who lost their hope in a certain moment of their lives.
